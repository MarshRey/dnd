### Overview

The Forge-Spires are three massive basalt towers built into the side of [[Mount_Ignis\|Mount_Ignis]], an active volcano. Because of the geothermal heat, the Stygian Creep cannot settle here. It is the only place in the known world where one can walk outside without a heavy coat.

### What You Have Heard

- **Heat is Currency:** In the Spires, you do not pay for bread; you pay for "Thermal Access." The closer you live to the volcanic vents, the higher your status.
    
- **The Monopoly:** They control the production of [[Soul_Coal\|Soul_Coal]]. The only fuel that can keep portable lanterns burning in the deep tundra.
    
- **The Refugee Crisis:** Thousands are camped at the base of the mountain. The Iron-Gate only opens for those with rare talents or deep pockets. Though some who can prove their worth are also let in.
    

**DM SECRETS: The Volcano's Price**

- **The Artificial Heat:** The volcano is being kept hot by a bound Fire Elemental of immense power. The elemental is dying, and the Spires are looking for a replacement—perhaps one of the players.
    
- **The Sabotage:** The leaders are secretly helping the Stygian_Creep spread. The more the world freezes, the more people are forced to buy their coal. They are war profiteers.
    
- **The Entry Hook:** The players’ caravan was heading here because they have vouchers or talents the Spires allegedly need.
