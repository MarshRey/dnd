### The Departure

The journey began at the Sunken Port, a skeletal remains of the continent’s greatest trade hub. You boarded the [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Factions/Cinder-Trail_Caravan\|Cinder-Trail_Caravan]], a line of four massive, steam-heated sleds designed to breach the growing wastes. For six days, the rhythm of the journey was defined by the mechanical thrum of the engines and the sight of the white horizon.

### The Ambush

On the sixth night, the temperature plummeted beyond the capacity of the caravan’s heaters. The steam in the pipes turned to ice instantly, causing the engines to groan and seize. Then came the silence of the tundra.

Out of the white cold, spindly, translucent figures known as [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Characters/NPC/Enemies/Stygian_Scavengers\|Stygian_Scavengers]] moved with impossible speed. The lead sled was flipped. The fourth sled was shredded. Your sled, the third, veered sharply, striking a shelf of jagged Black Ice. As the world tilted and the glass shattered, the last thing you saw was the blue, flickering glow of the scavengers closing in.
