### Power Structure

The city is governed by **The Circle of Thorns**, a council of high druids who interpret the "Slow Will" of the [[Giant_Ancient_Tortoises\|Giant_Ancient_Tortoises]]. Power is not held by the wealthy, but by the **Green-Singers**—specialized bards and druids whose songs keep the tortoises moving and the internal forests blooming.

### Goals

- **The South-Seed:** The druids believe they carry the "World-Seed," a magical artifact that can instantly grow a forest once they find soil untouched by [[Mechanics/Stygian_Frostbite\|Stygian_Frostbite]].
    
- **Ley-Line Migration:** Their primary objective is to keep the tortoises on ancient magical paths that provide the energy necessary to sustain the city's internal summer.
    

### What Makes Them Interesting

The city has no "buildings" in the traditional sense. Structures are woven from living wood. To keep the tortoises calm, a city-wide "Vow of Quiet" is enforced near the front of the caravan. Loud noises or mechanical vibrations are seen as an affront to the city's life force.
