### Power Structure

A strict meritocracy governed by **The Board of Gears**. Every citizen is assigned a "Cog-Rank" based on their technical skills. The higher your rank, the more heat-rations and better housing you receive. The **Skate-Captains** are the elite, responsible for navigating the massive adamantine blades across the shifting ice.

### Goals

- **Resource Dominance:** They seek to reach the [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Atlas/Cities/The_Forge-Spires\|The_Forge-Spires]] to secure a contract for the exclusive rights to [[Mechanics/Soul_Coal\|Soul_Coal]] mining.
    
- **The Steel Path:** They aim to build a permanent rail-system south, believing that only through industrial permanence can civilization survive.
    

### What Makes Them Interesting

Iron-Pivot is loud, soot-covered, and efficient. The city is a master of "Recycling." Nothing is wasted; broken tools, scrap metal, and even the deceased are repurposed to keep the "Chain-Gang" of Frost Giants moving. If a person becomes "Inoperable" (too sick or injured to work), they are often exiled to the wastes to save fuel.
