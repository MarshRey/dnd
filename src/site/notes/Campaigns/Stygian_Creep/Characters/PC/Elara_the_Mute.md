### Overview

Elara is a survivor of the [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Factions/Cinder-Trail_Caravan\|Cinder-Trail_Caravan]] wreckage and a Cleric of the Hearth. She is a woman in her late twenties with silver-streaked hair and eyes the color of dying embers. She wears heavy, soot-stained vestments over a suit of battered chainmail. She is currently afflicted by the [[Blue-Vein_Curse\|Blue-Vein_Curse]] curse, which has left her completely unable to speak.

### Personality and Role

Despite her silence, Elara is a grounding presence. She communicates through a series of practiced hand signals, sketches in the snow, and intense facial expressions. She acts as a guide for the party, as she has made the trek between The_Sunken_Port and The_Forge-Spires several times before.

- **The Healer:** She carries a holy symbol of a flickering flame. While she can provide basic healing, her powers are severely dampened by the cold.
    
- **The Protector:** She is fiercely protective of the Hearth-Lantern, treating it as a sacred object.
    

### The Blue-Vein Curse

Elara’s throat is laced with thin, glowing blue veins—a mark of the Stygian_Creep.

- **Mechanical Limitation:** Until her spirit is "thawed," she cannot cast spells with Verbal components. This limits her to basic restorative magic and physical defense.
    
- **Restoration:** She requires regular proximity to Soul_Coal heat to prevent the curse from spreading to her heart.
