### Description

Stygian Scavengers are the most common predators of the frozen wastes. They appear as gaunt, four limbed humanoids with skin like translucent, cracked ice. Their eyes glow with a dim, flickering blue light, and they move with a jerky, unnatural grace. They do not eat flesh in the traditional sense; they consume the heat and life-force of living creatures.
