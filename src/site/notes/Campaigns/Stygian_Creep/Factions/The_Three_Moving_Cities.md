# The Great Migration

To survive, civilization must stay ahead of the "Stygian Creep."

1. **[[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Atlas/Cities/Everbloom\|Everbloom]]:** A druidic forest city pulled by Ancient Tortoises.
    
2. **[[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Atlas/Cities/Iron-Pivot\|Iron-Pivot]]:** An industrial city on massive skates pulled by Frost Giants.
    
3. **[[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Atlas/Cities/Aetheria\|Aetheria]]:** A floating city of balloons and airships.
