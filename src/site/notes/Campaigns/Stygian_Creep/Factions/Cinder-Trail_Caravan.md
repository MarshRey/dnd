# Overview

The Cinder-Trail Caravan is the final operational transport line connecting the [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Atlas/Cities/The_Sunken_Port\|The_Sunken_Port]] to The_Forge-Spires. It is not a path, but a fleet of heavily reinforced, steam-powered sleds that navigate the shifting "Black Ice" of the wastes. What was once a weekly service with dozens of sleds has been reduced to a single, desperate monthly run.

### Technical Details

- **The Sleds:** These are massive, iron-shod vehicles the size of small houses. They move on wide, tank-like treads and are heated by internal boilers that burn massive amounts of Soul_Coal.
    
- **The Route:** The "Cinder Trail" refers to the layer of ash and soot left behind by the sleds' exhausts, which serves as a grim navigational marker in the whiteouts.
    
- **The Risk:** Every journey has a 40% casualty rate. Between mechanical failure in the extreme cold and attacks from [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Characters/NPC/Enemies/Stygian_Scavengers\|Stygian_Scavengers]], reaching the Spires is considered a miracle.
