**The Hearth of the World**

### Overview

Mount Ignis is a colossal, perpetually active volcano that serves as the geothermal anchor for the region. It is the only geographical feature visible for hundreds of miles, a jagged black pillar constantly venting orange-tinted smoke into the bruised, gray sky. Because of the intense heat radiating from its core, the Stygian_Creep cannot settle within its immediate vicinity, making it the most valuable piece of land left on the continent.

### Geography and Industry

The mountain is the site of [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Atlas/Cities/The_Forge-Spires\|The_Forge-Spires]], which are built directly into its basalt flanks. The lower slopes are riddled with mining shafts where workers extract [[Mechanics/Soul_Coal\|Soul_Coal]] from the fossilized remains of ancient fire giants.

- **The Heat-Wash:** The air around the mountain is thick with ash and sulfur. While it is warm, it is not "fresh"; the atmosphere is heavy and metallic.
    
- **The Lava-Veins:** Molten rock is diverted through massive stone conduits to power the forges and heating systems of the city.
