**The Cloud-Drifter Collective**

### Overview

Aetheria is a cluster of elegant, gold-leafed villas and towers suspended by hundreds of massive, silk-spun hot-air envelopes. It floats roughly 1,000 feet above the Stygian_Creep, staying within warmer air currents.

### The Engine

The city uses [[Arcane_Envelopes\|Arcane_Envelopes]] magical balloons filled with a lighter-than-air gas harvested from the "Gas-Giants," massive floating creatures that inhabit the upper atmosphere.

### What You Have Heard

- **The High-Born:** The people of Aetheria are scholars, mages, and the ultra-wealthy. They are said to be incredibly arrogant, looking down on the "Mud-Keepers" below.
    
- **The Stygian Winds:** Travelers say Aetheria is being hunted. Unnatural, freezing winds the "Breath of Levistus"—have been trying to blow the city north.
    
- **The Sky-Dock:** They rarely land. To get in, you must be winched up in a gilded cage, a terrifying ride through the freezing altitudes.
