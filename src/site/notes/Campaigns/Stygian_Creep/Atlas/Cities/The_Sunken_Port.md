**The Ghost of the Great City**

### Overview

Once the jewel of the southern coast and the seat of the continent's empire, the Sunken Port is now a necropolis of marble and ice. Most of the city is drowned frozen beneath twenty feet of supernatural ice that swept in from the sea. Only the highest spires and the fortified dockyards remain above the frost line.

### Current State

The city is effectively abandoned, save for the **Port District**, which is maintained by a skeleton crew of mercenaries and desperate dockworkers.

- **The Funnel:** This is the only place where ships from across the sea can still land. It is a chaotic, miserable bottleneck for thousands of refugees trying to secure a seat on the [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Factions/Cinder-Trail_Caravan\|Cinder-Trail_Caravan]].
    
- **The Frozen Echoes:** In the abandoned residential districts, the Stygian_Creep happened so fast that people were frozen in place. You can still see "statues" of citizens through the translucent ice of the streets.
