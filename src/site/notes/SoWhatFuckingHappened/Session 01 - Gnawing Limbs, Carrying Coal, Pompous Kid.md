The party woke up at sled 3, the first sled being the last sled in the line of the caravan.

They grouped up, introduced themselves a little but realized they had more pressing matters at hand. Its fucking cold and the dog is already eating frozen bodies.

After scolding the dog and fighting a Stygian Scavenger the part began moving towards the large volcanic city of the Forge Spires. 

Checking sled by sled, filled with dead and dread. But also loot.

A smoky imp scouted the path forward, while the party loaded bags of coal onto a flying pillow. 

Arriving at the lead caravan the players found a kid of unknown origins. Tortured him with duplicating dogs and thaumaturgical explosions. Having no chance of survival on his own he begrudgingly joins you on your journey to the forge spires. 



Tensions and relations between party members and their personalities continue to grow.

The dog wizard, [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Characters/PC/Plort-Milly\|Plort-Milly]], is mischievous but only the paladin,  [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Characters/PC/Darien-Dom\|Darien-Dom]], seems to be bothered by it.

The warlock, [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Characters/PC/William-Dalton\|William-Dalton]], seems to have a fascination with what is in the pants of frozen dead people... 
to each their own i guess.



Little is know about the final member,  [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Characters/PC/Adelore-Des\|Adelore-Des]]. They seem to have some musical talent and great utility with their pillow. Maybe over the next days of crossing the frozen tundra this mysterious member will 'add a lore' ;) about themself.

Huddled at the center of the party carrying the single chance of making it to the Forge Spires is [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Characters/PC/Elara_the_Mute\|Elara_the_Mute]]. The [[Mechanics/Hearth-Lantern\|Hearth-Lantern]] only works with [[Mechanics/Soul_Coal\|Soul_Coal]] which seems to be hard to distinguish and hard to find.

May the gods keep the party safe from the frost.
