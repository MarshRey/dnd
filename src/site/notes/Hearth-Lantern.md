### Description

The Hearth-Lantern is a heavy, iron-bound vessel roughly the size of a human head. Its glass panels are thick, leaded, and slightly tinted orange to withstand the extreme thermal expansion of [[Mechanics/Soul_Coal\|Soul_Coal]]. Unlike standard oil lanterns, it does not produce a large flame; instead, it houses a central iron grate where shards of coal are placed to glow with an intense, magical heat.

### Technical Specifications

- **Light:** Provides 20 feet of bright light and an additional 20 feet of dim light.
    
- **Heat Radius:** Generates a 10-foot radius of "Comfortable" temperature (roughly 65°F), even in a blizzard.

### Fuel and Usage

The lantern has **3 Fuel Slots**.

- **Fuel Source:** Only [[Mechanics/Soul_Coal\|Soul_Coal]] can power the lantern. One shard of coal fills one slot.
    
- **Consumption:** One slot is consumed every 8 hours of continuous use.
    
- **The Long Rest:** A party cannot gain the benefits of a Long Rest in the wastes unless the lantern is active for the duration of the rest.
    
- **Treatment:** Resting within the heat radius of an active lantern is the only way to recover Maximum HP lost to [[Mechanics/Stygian_Frostbite\|Stygian_Frostbite]].
