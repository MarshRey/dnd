In the Stygian Tundra, the environment is as much of an enemy as any monster. 

The Supply Die 
Starting Die: d12
When to Roll: After Long Rests or forced marches. 
Degradation: On a 1 or 2, the die drops a tier (d12 > d10 > d8 > d6 > d4).
Buy supplies in towns to increase die level

The Hearth-Lantern
Your primary source of heat.
It has 3 Fuel Slots
Consumption: 1 fuel per 8 hours of rest.
Recharging: Can be refilled with Soul_Coal found in the world.
Main method of curing Stygian_Frostbite
