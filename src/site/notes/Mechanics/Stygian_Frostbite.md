**Mechanic: The Lingering Cold**

Standard healing spells (`Cure Wounds`, `Healing Word`) and potions are effective against physical wounds, but they cannot mend the damage caused by Stygian cold.

### The Rule

When a creature takes Cold damage from a Stygian source , that damage is recorded as **Stygian Frostbite**.

- **Max HP Reduction:** The target’s Hit Point Maximum is reduced by an amount equal to the Stygian Frostbite damage taken.
    
- **Healing Restriction:** This reduction cannot be removed by magical healing or standard Long Rests.
    
- **The Cure:** To restore their Maximum Hit Points, a creature must spend a Long Rest within 10 feet of a generator or hearth-lantern powered by Soul_Coal.
