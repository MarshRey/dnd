### Description

Soul Coal is a dense, crystalline substance that glows with a deep, internal orange ember. Unlike standard coal, it does not crumble; it feels as heavy and cold as iron until ignited. When burned, it produces a steady, intense heat and a faint smell of sulfur and ancient stone.

### Origin

This fuel is harvested from the deep strata of [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Atlas/Location/Mount_Ignis\|Mount_Ignis]]. It is composed of the fossilized, carbonized remains of Fire Giants who perished within the mountain during the first great freezing. Their innate elemental essence remains trapped within the coal, providing the only heat source powerful enough to counteract the influence of Prince_Levistus.

### Mechanical Use

- **Fueling:** One shard of Soul Coal provides 8 hours of heat for a Hearth-Lantern or a small generator.
    
- **Healing:** Resting near a Soul Coal fire is the only way to recover from Stygian_Frostbite.
    
- **Availability:** Currently, [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Atlas/Cities/The_Forge-Spires\|The_Forge-Spires]] holds a strict monopoly on the excavation and distribution of Soul Coal.
