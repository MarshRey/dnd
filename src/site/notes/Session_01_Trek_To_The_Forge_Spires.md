### The Scene

The session opens with the players regaining consciousness amidst the twisted iron and hissing steam of the [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Factions/Cinder-Trail_Caravan\|Cinder-Trail_Caravan]]. The wreckage of Sled Three is partially buried in a snowdrift, providing a small pocket of shelter from the "Silent Snow" winds.

Elara the Cleric is holding the [[Mechanics/Hearth-Lantern\|Hearth-Lantern]] in front of the players

**The Objective:** Reach [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Atlas/Cities/The_Forge-Spires\|The_Forge-Spires]] before the [[Mechanics/Hearth-Lantern\|Hearth-Lantern]] runs out of fuel or the [[Mechanics/Stygian_Frostbite\|Stygian_Frostbite]] takes hold.
