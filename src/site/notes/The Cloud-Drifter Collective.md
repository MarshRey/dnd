### Power Structure

A feudal aristocracy led by **The High Navigators**. Bloodline is everything here; the families who owned the original airship fleets that formed the city still hold all the power. The lower classes live in the "Hanging Slums" platforms suspended beneath the main villas, exposed to the higher winds and freezing altitudes.

### Goals

- **Atmospheric Sovereignty:** They believe the ground is lost to [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Characters/NPC/Enemies/Prince_Levistus\|Prince_Levistus]]. Their goal is to find a way to reach the "Aether-Belt" a rumored layer of the upper atmosphere that is permanently warm.
    
- **Gas Harvesting:** They are constantly searching for "Sky-Whales" and other gas-producing creatures to replenish their envelopes.
    

### What Makes Them Interesting

Aetheria is a city of extreme verticality and snobbery. They view those on the ground as "Mud-Keepers" and rarely interact with the other factions except to trade for rare lumber from [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Atlas/Cities/Everbloom\|Everbloom]] or metal parts from [[Campaigns/Stygian_Creep/Atlas/Cities/Iron-Pivot\|Iron-Pivot]]. They do not land; all trade is conducted via "Winch-Buckets" and "Gilded Cages."
